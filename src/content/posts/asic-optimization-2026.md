As the block reward continues to shrink, mining efficiency isn't just an advantage—it's a requirement for survival. With hash price at all-time lows, we look at how to squeak every last terahash out of your ASIC hardware.

## Underclocking vs. Overclocking

In bull markets, everyone wants more TH/s. In bear markets, efficiency (J/TH) is king. 

### Advanced Firmware
By deploying custom firmware like Braiins OS+ or LuxOS, you can enable auto-tuning. This process finds the optimal frequency for every single chip on your hashboards. 

* Increases overall lifespan of the hardware
* Reduces heat output significantly
* Can improve J/TH efficiency by up to 15%

### Immersion Cooling
Air cooling is out, immersion is in. Submerging your ASICs in dielectric fluid not only dampens the deafening noise, but also allows for safe 20%+ overclocks without degrading the chips.
